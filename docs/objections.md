## Format

Each objection entry:
- **Objection:** The exact or paraphrased objection
- **Persona:** Who raises it most often
- **Root concern:** What fear or constraint is underneath the objection
- **Response:** Suggested counter-framing
- **Open question:** What we still need to learn

## Detection comparison

### "Why not just use Turnitin or GPTZero?"
- Persona: Faculty, Provost
- Root concern: Familiar tool, perceived sufficiency
- Response: Acta replaces detection with evidence. Detection tools have accuracy and legal
  liability problems. Acta produces a signed ledger that defends the grade on appeal — something
  no detection tool can do.
- Open question: What detection tool failures have they personally experienced?
